Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password when you create an account. We also collect the expense data you log, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions, so that we can display your spending back to you.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate and improve Spendly: authenticating your account, storing and displaying your expenses, and generating the summaries and breakdowns you see in the app. We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored in our database for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time, after which your data will be permanently removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may rely on trusted infrastructure providers, such as hosting and email delivery services, solely to operate the application, and these providers are bound to handle data securely and only as needed to provide their service to us.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website. We're happy to address any concerns about your data.
